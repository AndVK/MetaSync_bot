# MetaSync Bot — Simple Crypto Price Tracker in Telegram

**MetaSync Bot** is a straightforward Telegram bot that tracks Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), and Solana (SOL) prices.  
No clutter. No complexity. Just prices and alerts when you need them.

👉 **Try the bot:** [@MetaSync_bot](https://t.me/MetaSync_bot)

---

## Why simple?

Most crypto bots are overloaded with features, charts, and noise. **MetaSync Bot** does one thing well:  
it keeps you updated on prices with zero friction.

- **No setup** — just press Start
- **No accounts** — works with your Telegram
- **No distractions** — only what matters: prices and alerts

---

## What it does

- **Daily reports** — every day at 12:00 UTC, get current prices and 24h changes for BTC, ETH, SOL
- **Instant alerts** — receive notifications when any coin price moves more than 1%
- **On-demand prices** — use `/price` to check current rates anytime
- **Flexible settings** — enable or disable volatility alerts for each coin with `/change`

---

## Bot Commands

| Command | What it does |
|---------|--------------|
| `/start` | Subscribe to daily reports |
| `/stop` | Unsubscribe from reports |
| `/price` | Show current BTC, ETH, SOL prices |
| `/change` | Configure volatility alerts per coin |
| `/help` | Show available commands |

---

## How to get started

1. Open the bot: [@MetaSync_bot](https://t.me/MetaSync_bot)
2. Tap **Start** (or send `/start`)
3. You're subscribed. That's it.
